Standard Operating Procedure

Title: Forklift Unloading Procedure

Purpose

This procedure outlines the safe method for unloading palletised freight using a forklift within the ELX depot environment to ensure safety and compliance with workplace health and safety requirements.

Scope

This SOP applies to all warehouse staff and forklift operators handling palletised freight deliveries.

Procedure

  1. Conduct pre-start inspection of forklift equipment.
  2. Establish exclusion zone around the unloading area.
  3. Remove load restraints carefully from trailer.
  4. Use forklift to unload pallets and move them to the staging area.

Hazards & Controls

Falling loads: Ensure pallets are stable before lifting and maintain safe load handling practices.

Pedestrian interaction: Maintain exclusion zone and ensure a spotter is present where required.

Required PPE

Hi-vis clothing, steel cap boots, gloves, and other site-required PPE.

Emergency Response

Stop work immediately, isolate the area, notify the supervisor, and follow ELX emergency procedures.
